Island Overview

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific with varied terrain, beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and a small active volcano. The island is home to about 20,000 people and offers a wide range of activities and accommodations for visitors.

Frequently Requested Information

  • Power outlets: 120 volts (US standard)
  • Currency: US Dollar (USD), also accepts euros and yen
  • Languages: Tanitian, English (in cities)
  • Emergency: 911
  • Alcohol not served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • Drinking age: 18 (not strictly enforced)
  • Hospital and clinics available; hospital staff are multilingual
  • Violent crime is rare, but watch for pickpocketing
  • Many attractions/restaurants closed on national holidays
