About Taniti

Taniti is a family-friendly destination. For those traveling with children, enjoy shallow, safe beach waters and kid-friendly dining options.

Transportation

Getting around Taniti is easy with a variety of transportation options. Below are details on how to get from the airport or cruise ship terminal to your hotel:

From Airport or Cruise Ship Terminal

- Shuttle services are available right outside the terminal with fares starting at $15.
- Taxis cost approximately $20 for a one-way trip.
- Public buses run every 30 minutes and cost $2.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do in an emergency?

In case of an emergency, call 911 for local services. For tourist assistance, dial 123-456-7890.

Are there child-friendly attractions?

Yes! Visit the family-friendly beaches with shallow waters, the Taniti Zoo, and the Children's Adventure Park.

Are the restaurants family-friendly?

Many restaurants in Taniti offer kid-friendly meals. Look for local favorites like The Island Cafe.

Food Options

Explore Taniti's local dining options:

  • Ocean's Grill: Famous for fresh seafood and seaside dining.
  • The Island Cafe: A family-friendly restaurant with kid menus and local specialties.
  • Taniti Street Food Market: Try authentic dishes like grilled fish tacos and tropical smoothies.
