Explore Taniti’s Top Attractions

White Sand Beaches

Relax or play on the beautiful beaches surrounding Yellow Leaf Bay. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and family fun.

Active Volcano

Visit Taniti’s small, active volcano for a truly unique experience. Guided tours available for all ages.

History Museum

Learn about Taniti’s rich history and culture at the local museum.

Ocean Adventures

Enjoy stunning ocean views and boat tours around the island.

Merriton Landing

This lively area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay features pubs, a microbrewery, dance club, arcade, bowling, and family-friendly entertainment.

Plan Your Visit

Many attractions are open year-round, but check for holiday closures. Guided tours are recommended for the volcano and rainforest.
