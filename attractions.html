Explore Taniti’s Top Attractions

White Sand Beaches

Relax or play on the beautiful beaches surrounding Yellow Leaf Bay. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and family fun.

Lush Rainforest

Take a guided hike or try zip-lining through Taniti’s tropical rainforest. Great for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Active Volcano

Visit Taniti’s small, active volcano for a truly unique experience. Guided tours available for all ages.

Merriton Landing

This lively area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay features pubs, a microbrewery, dance club, arcade
